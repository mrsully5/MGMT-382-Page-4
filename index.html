Eloheh Healthcare Services

Health support that’s personal, professional, and always within reach.

What We Offer

  • In-home care support and mobility assistance
  • Remote telehealth consultations
  • Medical device training and setup
  • Caregiver coaching and resources
  • Ongoing education and wellness integration

Steps to Get Started

  1. Request service through email or website
  2. Speak with a care specialist to assess needs
  3. Receive your personalized plan and equipment
  4. Begin services with setup help and training
  5. Enjoy 24/7 access to support

Personalized Wellness Support

We provide individualized guidance and one-on-one care planning to help patients choose and learn to use the right tools.

Telehealth & Remote Support

Virtual care is available when you need it most. Our licensed professionals offer full setup help and check-ins through secure video consultations.

Medical Equipment Coaching

We don’t just deliver — we ensure proper and safe use through personalized instruction and support.

Full Program Integration

Our team coordinates with your doctors, physical therapists, and family to align everything in one care plan.

Ongoing Learning Resources

Caregivers and families can access online tools, printed guides, and coaching to stay informed and confident.

Service Affordability

We help maximize insurance benefits and offer discounts. Our goal: no one should skip care because of cost.
